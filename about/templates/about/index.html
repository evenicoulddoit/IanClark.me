“Hi, I'm Ian, thanks for visting.”

Hello and welcome to my web site. My name's Ian Clark , and I'm a web developer from the UK. I currently work for MyOptique Group as a Front-end Developer, helping to deliver the Glasses Direct website amongst other things. My role requires a strong knowledge of Python, HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and I have experience in a number of other languages and technologies . The aims of this site are twofold: to document my hobbies and skills, and to deliver useful resources from which others can learn. View the codebase on GitHub.

My passion for web development

I began developing websites at the age of 14, in 2005. My initial static templates quickly evolved into dynamic and interactive content, and before long, I found myself developing educational content which received millions of hits , and which was being cited as a pre-requisite for university modules. Shortly after completing my GCSEs, I was even featured in a local newspaper for developing an impartial electronic reviews website (highly photogenic!).

Whilst my skills have progressed considerably since my teenage years, I'm still as excited as I ever have been to deliver experiences which users love, and to help others develop a similar passion .

Other interests

I'm actually a graduate in Economics, achieving a First-class degree from Loughborough University. I chose to study Economics, because I'm particularly interested in both its behavioural and welfare aspects. I also love science and listening to podcasts, such as RadioLab, Freakonomics and TED .

Outside of academia, I'm interested in world news, photography (though not especially good at it), and sports such as football, weightlifting and badminton.
